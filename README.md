A campus platform where students share resources and split bike rides to save money and reduce waste.


## What It Does

- **Share Resources** - Post/browse items like books, cycles, electronics, furniture
- **Share Rides** - Offer/find bike rides, split costs with other students
- **Search & Filter** - Find what you need quickly
- **100% Free** - No cost to use
